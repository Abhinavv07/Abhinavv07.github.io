Gta Vice City

This was the first game that I had played.Grand Theft Auto: Vice City is a 2002 action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games.The single-player story follows mobster Tommy Vercetti's rise to power after being released from prison and becoming caught up in an ambushed drug deal.

Gta san andreas

Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas is an action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It was initially released on October 26, 2004.The game is set in the fictional state of San Andreas, which is heavily inspired by California and Nevada."San Andreas" is known for its open-world gameplay, which allows players to explore a vast and detailed environment, engage in various missions and activities, and interact with the game world and its inhabitants. The game offers a mix of on-foot and vehicular gameplay, along with elements like shooting, driving, melee combat, and role-playing game mechanics such as character customization, skills, and stat-building.
